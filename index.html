About Me

After immigrating to Los Angeles from Seoul, I graduated from University of California, Santa Barbara. I was pursuing a career in physical therapy while I worked as a ride-share gig driver to support myself. During this time, I serendipitously met a passenger who struck up a welcome and interesting conversation during our ride. She encouraged me to explore a career as a software engineer and provided me with useful online resources. After doing further research that week, I was hooked. In April 2020, I graduated from UCLA Extension's Full Stack Web Development program.

I have a year of experience as a volunteer front-end engineer at Omou Learning , a tutoring management service designed for remote learning. Outside of web development, you can find me geeking out on biomechanics and putting my experience as a physical therapy tech and NASM Personal Trainer certification to use by offering practical support to maximize the long-term health benefits of strength training to those around me.

I am committed to learning from and contributing to an inclusive team. If you are currently looking to fill a role, please feel free to contact me through the tab above to discuss or receive my resume.
